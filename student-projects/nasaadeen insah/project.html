Our Story

The Fresh Approach to Communication

Founded in 2021 in London's iconic West End, we noticed that corporate communications had lost their flavor. They were dry, predictable, and easily ignored. We decided to shake things up.

By blending the rich, juicy creative storytelling of a boutique design house (**the strawberry**) with the precise, high-visibility tactical logic of modern PR metrics (**the touch of yellow**), we deliver campaigns that don't just sit there—they spark connections.
